Shri Gajanan Maharaj Sansthan- Trimbak, Maharashtra

Nestled in the serene town of Trimbak, Maharashtra, Shri Gajanan Maharaj Sansthan stands as a beacon of spiritual devotion and cultural heritage. Dedicated to Shri Gajanan Maharaj, a revered saint known for his divine wisdom and miraculous powers, the Sansthan has become a sacred destination for devotees from all walks of life.

The temple is not only a place of worship but also a center for social welfare and spiritual education. With its tranquil surroundings and a sense of profound spirituality, the Sansthan offers a peaceful retreat for those seeking solace, guidance, and blessings.
